Overview

When a planet crosses in front of its star, brightness dips periodically. But stellar activity, instrument noise, and gaps make detection hard. We combine transit search with AI to score candidates and present uncertainty.

Objectives

  • Preprocess light curves (detrend, normalize, gap-safe resample).
  • Search periodic dips (Box-Least Squares / Transit Least Squares).
  • Classify candidates with a compact 1D-CNN + tabular features.
  • Explain predictions and surface vetting checks.

Key Outputs

  • Period, depth, duration, SNR.
  • Phase-folded curve & model confidence.
  • Quality flags and false-positive vetoes.
  • Downloadable report.
02

Data & Preprocessing

Compatible with public mission data (e.g., Kepler/TESS-style light curves). You can demo with any CSV of time, flux.

Pipeline

1. Clean

Remove NaNs/outliers, interpolate small gaps.

2. Detrend

High-pass filter or spline to remove stellar variability.

3. Search

BLS/TLS grid for candidate periods & epochs.

4. Features

Depth, duration, SNR, odd-even test, secondary eclipse check.

04

Model at a Glance

Compact 1D-CNN Classifier

Processes normalized segments around candidate events. Outputs calibrated confidence (0–1) and uncertainty band.

// Pseudocode (concept)
// x: normalized flux segment
// y: probability of transit
Conv1D(16,k=5) → ReLU → MaxPool
Conv1D(32,k=5) → ReLU → GlobalAvgPool
Concat(tabular_features)
Dense(64) → Dropout → Dense(1, sigmoid)

Feature Highlights

  • Depth, duration, SNR, transit shape metrics.
  • Odd–even, secondary eclipse, centroid shift checks.
  • Period aliases & harmonics handling.

Results & Insights

Benchmarks

On curated datasets, our pipeline targets higher recall on low-SNR signals with competitive precision after veto rules.

  • Improved detection on short-duration transits.
  • Lower false positives from stellar variability.
  • Human-interpretable vetting artifacts.

What’s Next

  • Richer uncertainty quantification.
  • Active-learning with community feedback.
  • Extended support for more missions.
